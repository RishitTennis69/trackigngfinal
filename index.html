FAQ

Quick answers before you scan.

What AI platforms do you track?

Gleo currently checks ChatGPT, Gemini, and Claude, with the scan results separated by platform.

What does the scan measure?

It measures mention visibility, first-choice share, average rank, competitor presence, and sentiment.

How are prompts generated?

Gleo reads the site first, then builds customer-style prompts around the business, service categories, local intent, and nearby comparison searches.

Can I export or share results?

Not yet. Gleo is focused on tracking how AI mentions and ranks your business inside the dashboard.
